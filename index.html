The ARCs of the JOURNEY - June 12 Update

Heal yourself, with beautiful love and always remember.. you are the medicine. -Maria Sabina

In the psychedelic assisted therapy realm, there is obviously a lot of focus on the psychedelics themselves and the medicine ceremony. But this is only part of the journey, and the arc of the entire journey is much grander. Instead of grasping onto a particular medicine, ceremony, framework, guru, etc., I put forth these principles as a gentle reminder that the skills and mindset that help you prepare for, navigate, and integrate a psychedelic experience, are the same skills and mindset that allow you to be the medicine, for yourself, everyone and everything around you, for the rest of your life.

Preparation ↔ Contentment

Sending out invitations, rather than intentions, which can easily morph into expectations. Not grasping onto outcomes; trusting that what comes up is an essential part of the journey.

If you want to catch a horse, don't chase it. The distance will only get further.- I ching

Ceremony ↔ Contentment

When we let go of attachments, we are able to fully engage with what comes to us, experiencing it with a freshness and openness, feeling fully, with the ability to be present with whatever arises; no judgment, only wonder.

Integration ↔ Contentment

Losing the linear growth check-list mindset. Healing happens in a spiral, uncovering layers and coming back around. Grasping at hopes, fears, and regrets keeps you out of the present, where you are most open to growth. having self-compassion, being grateful for small steps.
